SHADOWED RIDGE

SHADOWED RIDGE follows a group of friends who travel to a remote mountain cabin for a quiet weekend getaway. What starts as a relaxing escape quickly turns into a nightmare when they realize they are being watched. As strange events unfold, they discover the area’s dark past involving a brutal killer who never truly left. Cut off from help and surrounded by dense forest, the friends must rely on their instincts and trust each other to survive the night. The film blends psychological tension with intense survival horror, keeping viewers on edge until the final reveal.
