Online Battle Royale Games

Online battle royale games are multiplayer video games where many players compete in a large arena, starting with limited resources and battling until only one player or team remains. They combine survival, strategy, and fast-paced action, often featuring shrinking play zones, looting for weapons, and real-time competition with players from around the world.

Free Fire

Free Fire is a popular online battle royale mobile game developed by Garena, where players are dropped onto an island to fight for survival against other players. The game features fast-paced matches, character abilities, and a wide variety of weapons, with the goal of being the last player or team standing.

Call of Duty Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile is an online first-person shooter game that features an intense battle royale mode alongside classic multiplayer action. In the battle royale mode, players land on a large map, collect weapons and equipment, and fight to survive until the last player or team remains.

Farlight

Farlight is a fast-paced online battle royale game that combines futuristic combat with jetpacks and advanced weapons. Players battle on large maps, using mobility skills and strategy to outlast opponents and become the last player or team standing.
